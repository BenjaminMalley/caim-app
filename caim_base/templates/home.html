We’re officially testing our platform!

We’re officially testing our platform with rescue partners in certain counties in TX, NM and CO. This is a closed testing group to help us perfect our platform and get it ready for people across the US to use. By becoming a test partner you play a pivotal role in ensuring our platform is working well and truly gives rescues the functionality they need to be able to rescue more animals.

Here’s a list of our test counties:

  • Colorado counties: El Paso, Teller, Elbert, Douglas, Jefferson, Denver, Arapahoe
  • Texas counties: Potter, Randall, Hale, Armstrong
  • New Mexico county: Valencia

Info for people interested in fostering

If you live in one of the counties listed above in Texas or Colorado and are willing to become one of our first fosters, you can complete the foster application here. The shelter we’re testing with in New Mexico doesn’t have a foster program thus we’re waiting to bring on fosters there until we have a broader roll out.

If you don’t live in one of the counties listed but want to become a life-saving foster in your area, please sign up here and we’ll notify you as soon as we’re live in your city.

If you’d like to learn more about fostering, check out our fostering FAQs here.
